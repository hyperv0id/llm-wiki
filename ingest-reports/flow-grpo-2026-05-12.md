## 核心公式记录（摘要）

1. 流匹配目标：\( \mathcal{L}(\theta) = \mathbb{E}_{t,x_0,x_1}\|x_1-x_0 - v_\theta((1-t)x_0+tx_1,t)\|^2 \)
2. GRPO 优势估计：\( \hat{A}_i^t = (R_i - \mu_R)/\sigma_R \)
3. Flow-GRPO 损失：\( \mathcal{J} = \mathbb{E}[\min(r\hat{A},\text{clip}(r,1-\varepsilon,1+\varepsilon)\hat{A}) - \beta D_{\text{KL}}] \)
4. ODE-to-SDE 逆向 SDE：\( dx_t = (v_t - \frac{\sigma_t^2}{2}\nabla\log p_t)dt + \sigma_t dw \)
5. 速度场-得分关系：\( \nabla\log p_t(x) = -\frac{x}{t} - \frac{1-t}{t}v_t(x) \)
6. 最终 SDE：\( dx_t = [v_t + \frac{\sigma_t^2}{2t}(x_t + (1-t)v_t)]dt + \sigma_t dw \)
7. Euler-Maruyama 离散化：\( x_{t+\Delta t} = x_t + [v_\theta + \frac{\sigma_t^2}{2t}(x_t + (1-t)v_\theta)]\Delta t + \sigma_t\sqrt{\Delta t}\,\epsilon \)
8. KL 闭式解：\( D_{\text{KL}} = \frac{\|x_{t+\Delta t,\theta} - x_{t+\Delta t,\text{ref}}\|^2}{2\sigma_t^2\Delta t} \)
